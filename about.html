Who We Are

Welcome to Vijay Marble Industries, a leading provider of green marble and granite products. Established in the year 2000, our company has a rich history of providing top-quality products to customers all over the world. Our office is located in the beautiful city of Rajsamand, and our state-of-the-art polishing unit is situated in the city of Udaipur.

At Vijay Marble Industries, we believe in delivering the best of nature to our customers. Our green marble and granite products are a testament to our commitment to excellence and attention to detail. Our products are sourced from some of the finest quarries in India, and are carefully selected and processed to meet the highest standards of quality.

Our polishing unit in Udaipur is equipped with the latest technology and machinery, ensuring that our products receive the best finish and polish. Our team of skilled craftsmen takes great pride in their work, and are dedicated to ensuring that each product is finished to the highest standards of quality.

Over the years, Vijay Marble Industries has become synonymous with reliability and quality. We have a reputation for delivering products that are not only aesthetically pleasing, but also durable and long-lasting. Our products are used in a wide range of applications, from flooring and countertops, to walls and columns, and much more.

At Vijay Marble Industries, we believe that customer satisfaction is the key to success. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the best possible products and services, and always go the extra mile to ensure that their needs are met. Whether you are a builder, contractor, or homeowner, you can count on us to deliver the best products and services in the industry.

In conclusion, Vijay Marble Industries is a company that is dedicated to delivering the best of nature to its customers. With our focus on quality, customer service, and innovation, we are poised to be the leading provider of green marble and granite products for many years to come.

Currently we are dealing in All type of Indian Green Marble, R-Black Granite, Kotda Black Granite, South Galaxy Black Granite, P-white Granite, Red Granite, Brown Granite, Z-BLack Granite, Cheema Granite, S-White Granite Lapatra Granite, Madka brown Granite, Madka Black Granite.
