Product Detail

Rajasthan Black Granite is a wonderful name for many reasons. Its color is black and shiny, it looks like the night sky with glittering stars. It is also very rare and mostly only found in one place in India, Rajasthan.

It is black granite with blue and black small dots in the black background of the stone. Therefore, this variety of granite is a perfect fit for the house’s interior flooring and wall decoration. It has many names like Black Beauty, R Black, Ash Black, and so on. It is also, a perfect alternative to Absolute Black and Black galaxy granite because of its similar looks and is less expensive than other black granite. This type of black granite is discovered in the northern region of India. Most of its mines are located in the state of Rajasthan.

Rajasthan Black Granite is perfect for Exterior as well as interior walls and floor applications, countertops, stairs, window sills, mosaic, monuments, etc. Generally, you may consider this granite as a good option for countertops and flooring. When place in the right direction, this granite makes beautiful patterns. The stylish appearance complements the modern-day home and stainless steel appliances.
