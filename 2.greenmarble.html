Product Detail

Indian Green Marble has a smooth come with dark and lighter green appearance. Its deep green background is beautifully accented with lighter green veining and patches gives it a rich and inviting feeling.

Green marble is a natural stone with a unique green color and distinctive veining patterns. It is formed from sedimentary rock, often containing minerals such as serpentine or chlorite, which give it its color.

Green marble is a durable option, making it practical for high-traffic areas such as bathrooms and kitchens, as it is resistant to water, heat, and scratches. Its natural beauty and uniqueness make it a popular choice for high-end interiors, as it can complement a range of design styles.

Green marble is marked by the unique features of the light and dark shades with color variations, speckles, dots, waves, and flecks over the surface.

Its versatility and durability make it a popular choice for both residential and commercial applications.

Physical and chemical properties of a green marble product make it a superb choice for countertops, floor tiles, wall claddings, stairs, sculptures, and bathroom vanity tops at homes and offices.

Overall, green marble is a stunning and practical natural stone that can elevate the style and value of any interior design project.
